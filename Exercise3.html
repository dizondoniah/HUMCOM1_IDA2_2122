HOW TO COOK CHICKEN ADOBO

Ingridients and Tools:

    Ingridients

    • 4 to 5 pounds chicken
    • 1/2 cup white vinegar
    • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    • 4 cloves gralic and 5 garlic cloves crushed
    • 1 Onions
    • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
    • 1 1/2 cup water

    Tools

    • 2 pans
    • Large pot
    • Ladle

Procedure:

    Preparation

  1. Combine the chicken, 1/4 cup vinegar, 1/4 cup soy sauce, 4 garlic cloves in a large pot.
  2. Cover and marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for 1 to 3 hours.

    Cooking

  1. Heat oil in pan and crushed garlic and onions.
  2. Add 1 1/2 cup water and add the marinated chicken
  3. Add 1/4 soy sauce,1/4 vinegar and peppercorns
  4. Bring the chicken to a boil over high heat for 5-7min
  5. Lower the heat, cover and let simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Stir occasionally.
  7. Remove the lid and simmer until the sauce is reduced and thickened.
  8. Wait till the chicken is tender.
  9. Remove from the pot and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
